Our Process From Start to Finish

1

The prospect makes an enquiry from one of our adverts

You may come across our land projects via Google or a Facebook advertisement. Once you've clicked through to our website, you'll be able to view all available Land lots. If interested in any of our land projects, you will fill out a short contact form, which allows one of our agents at Oasis Land Development to contact you.

The prospect will receive a welcome email containing our current lots

Once you have filled out this enquiry form, you will receive a welcome email from our managing director "John B Mclean Jr". This email will include a link to our current lots available. Each individual lot includes more information about the location, nearby area & all the relevant due diligence documentation.

One of our agents will then give you a phone call

Within a few days of enquiring one of our agents will reach out to you. Initially, this will be an informal chat where no major decisions will be expected from you. This is simply an opportunity for you and our agent to get to know each other and answer any questions you may have about purchasing our land, & The Cayman Islands. This is also an opportunity for our agent to find out more about what you are looking for & tailor some of the options and information they send over to you.

One of our agents will then give you a phone call

You may wish to schedule a number of callbacks & ask a number of questions over email or a messaging service, which our agent is more than happy to do. Should you decide to move forward, all you need to do is let our agent know you would like to proceed. Once you proceed they will ask you to place a refundable reservation fee which will hold your chosen lot of the market for 7 days. This gives you more than enough time to once again go over all the provided information, and check over any due diligence documentation with the help of a lawyer. The reservation fee is completely refundable should you decide to not go ahead.

One of our agents will then give you a phone call

Again, if happy to proceed you will need to provide Oasis Land Development with your ID so we can conduct an AML (anti-money laundering check). Oasis Land Development will also issue you with all the draft contracts so they can be reviewed by yourself and your legal team.

One of our agents will then give you a phone call

Once you're happy with everything and have signed the contracts, all that's left to do is pay your agreed deposit. Once paid, the agreement will then go to The Cayman Islands for full exchange under Cayman Island law.
